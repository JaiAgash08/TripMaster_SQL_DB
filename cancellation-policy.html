Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances can change. Please review our cancellation policy below:

1. Cancellation Requests

All cancellation requests must be submitted via email to our customer service team at support@tripmaster.com.

2. Refund Policy

  • More than 30 days before the tour: Full refund minus a 10% processing fee.
  • 15 to 30 days before the tour: 50% refund of the total tour cost.
  • Less than 15 days before the tour: No refund.

3. Rescheduling

Guests may reschedule their tour once without incurring a fee, provided the request is made at least 15 days before the tour date. Rescheduling is subject to availability.

4. Tour Changes by TripMaster

We reserve the right to cancel or modify tours for any reason. In such cases, guests will be notified and offered a full refund or the option to reschedule.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our cancellation policy, please feel free to reach out to us through our Contact Us page.
